**Privacy Policy for KBinder**

Last updated: August 5, 2026
Version: 1.0.0

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

---

### 1. Information We Collect

KBinder is designed to respect your privacy.

We **do not collect, store, or share any personal data on external servers**.

However, the App may access the following on your device:

* **Photos and Media**: to allow you to select and store images of your cards
* **Camera**: to scan cards using your device camera
* **Local Storage**: to save your binders, images, and preferences

All data is stored **locally on your device only** and is not transmitted externally.

---

### 2. How We Use Information

The information accessed is used only to provide core app functionality:

* Organizing your digital card binders
* Saving and displaying images
* Allowing you to scan and manage cards
* Marking favorite cards

We do **not use your data for tracking, analytics, advertising, or profiling**.

---

### 3. Data Sharing

We do **not share, sell, or transfer your data to third parties**.

No personal or sensitive data is transmitted outside your device.

---

### 4. Data Security

Your data is stored locally on your device using standard system storage.

While we take reasonable measures to protect your data, no method of storage is completely secure.

---

### 5. Data Retention

All data remains on your device until you:

* Delete a card or binder within the App
* Uninstall the App (which may remove all stored data)

---

### 6. Children's Privacy

KBinder does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13.

Since no personal data is collected externally, the App is suitable for general audiences.

---

### 7. Permissions

The App may request the following permissions:

* Camera (to scan cards)
* Photos/Media (to select images from your gallery)
* Storage (to save data locally)

These permissions are used strictly to enable app functionality.

---

### 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date.
